Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Souq Albalad account at any time.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Souq Albalad app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

When your account is deleted, your personal information and associated data will be removed, except information that must be retained for legal or security purposes.
